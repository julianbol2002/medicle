CASE_ID: 001
DIFFICULTY: psychodle
SYSTEM: psychiatry_mood_disorders
DIAGNOSIS:
Major Depressive Disorder
ALIASES:
depression
clinical depression
MDD
VIGNETTE_LINES:
- A 28-year-old presents with persistent low mood, loss of interest in activities, and fatigue lasting over two months.
- The patient reports difficulty sleeping, poor concentration, and feelings of worthlessness.
- Appetite has decreased and there has been unintentional weight loss.
- No history of manic episodes is reported.
- Symptoms cause significant impairment in daily functioning.
TEACHING_POINTS:
- MDD requires at least 2 weeks of depressive symptoms with functional impairment.
- Anhedonia and low mood are key diagnostic features.
- Always assess for suicidality and risk factors.

CASE_ID: 002
DIFFICULTY: psychodle
SYSTEM: psychiatry_anxiety
DIAGNOSIS:
Generalized Anxiety Disorder
ALIASES:
GAD
chronic anxiety
VIGNETTE_LINES:
- A 35-year-old reports excessive worry about multiple aspects of life for over 6 months.
- The patient experiences restlessness, muscle tension, and difficulty concentrating.
- Sleep is disturbed with frequent awakenings.
- The worry is difficult to control and not limited to one specific trigger.
- Symptoms interfere with work and relationships.
TEACHING_POINTS:
- GAD involves excessive uncontrollable worry lasting at least 6 months.
- Physical symptoms like muscle tension and sleep disturbance are common.
- Cognitive behavioral therapy is a first-line treatment alongside SSRIs.

CASE_ID: 003
DIFFICULTY: psychodle
SYSTEM: psychiatry_psychotic
DIAGNOSIS:
Schizophrenia
ALIASES:
psychotic disorder
chronic schizophrenia
VIGNETTE_LINES:
- A 22-year-old presents with auditory hallucinations and paranoid delusions.
- The patient believes others are plotting against them and reports hearing voices commenting on actions.
- Social withdrawal and reduced emotional expression are noted.
- Symptoms have persisted for over 6 months.
- Functioning in school and work has significantly declined.
TEACHING_POINTS:
- Schizophrenia requires at least 6 months of symptoms including psychosis.
- Positive symptoms include hallucinations and delusions.
- Negative symptoms include flat affect and social withdrawal.

CASE_ID: 004
DIFFICULTY: psychodle
SYSTEM: psychiatry_trauma
DIAGNOSIS:
Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
ALIASES:
PTSD
trauma disorder
VIGNETTE_LINES:
- A 30-year-old presents after a traumatic event with recurrent nightmares and flashbacks.
- The patient avoids reminders of the trauma and feels persistently on edge.
- Sleep disturbances and irritability are present.
- Symptoms have lasted for several months after the event.
- There is significant impairment in daily functioning.
TEACHING_POINTS:
- PTSD includes intrusion, avoidance, negative mood, and hyperarousal.
- Symptoms must last more than 1 month.
- Trauma-focused therapy is first-line treatment.

DIFFICULTY: psychodle
SYSTEM: psychiatry_personality
DIAGNOSIS:
Borderline Personality Disorder
ALIASES:
BPD
emotion dysregulation disorder
VIGNETTE_LINES:
- A 24-year-old presents with intense unstable relationships and fear of abandonment.
- The patient reports impulsive behaviors and frequent mood swings.
- Episodes of self-harm and chronic feelings of emptiness are described.
- Identity disturbance and unstable self-image are noted.
- Interpersonal conflicts are frequent and severe.
TEACHING_POINTS:
- BPD involves instability in relationships, self-image, and emotions.
- Impulsivity and self-harm are common features.
- Dialectical behavior therapy is a key treatment approach.
